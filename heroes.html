Chaos Knight

Chaos Bolt

Throws a mysterious bolt of energy at the target unit. It stuns for a random duration and deals random damage.


Reality Rift

Teleports you, any images you have and the target unit to a random point along the line between the two of you. Reduces the target's armor for 8 seconds.


Chaos Strike

Each of Chaos Knight's attacks has a chance to deal bonus damage, also causing the critical strike to lifesteal for 75% of the damage.


Phantasm

Summons several phantasmal copies of the Chaos Knight from alternate dimensions. There is a 50% chance an extra phantasm will be summoned. The phantasms deal 100% damage, but take 260% damage. Upgradeable by Aghanim's Scepter. DISPEL TYPE: Basic Dispel
